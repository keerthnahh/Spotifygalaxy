03 — Flourish · Heatmap

THE PULSE OF GENRE

This streamgraph visualizes the 'heartbeat' of different musical genres. The horizontal axis (x-axis) represents Tempo, measured in Beats Per Minute (BPM), grouped into 10-beat 'bins.' Each colorful stream represents a unique genre. The vertical thickness of a stream indicates the volume of songs within that speed range. By observing the 'swell' and 'shrink' of these shapes, we can see where different styles of music naturally live. For instance, notice how high-energy genres like EDM or House expand significantly around the 120-130 BPM mark, while Hip-Hop and Acoustic styles occupy the slower, steadier currents on the left side of the river.

04 — Flourish · Chart

THE TIKTOK EFFECT: IS SHORTER BETTER?

Have you noticed songs getting shorter lately? This chart investigates if brevity is the secret to a hit. By plotting song length against popularity, we can see if the biggest tracks are actually the 'short and sweet' ones. Look for the cluster of hits around the 2-to-3-minute mark — that's the 'sweet spot' where music meets our shorter attention spans. It shows us that in the world of streaming, every second counts.
